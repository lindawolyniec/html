Architecture

The "new" church building was completed in 1952. The architect were Aloys Frank Herman and Howard Thomas Simons. the builder was Matthew Lalewicz, Inc.

History

Established in 1917, Queen of Apostles was built when Hamtramck was still a village on 32 parcels of land. The current church was completed in 1952.
